About Us

Paradise – A Centre for Development of Specially Abled Children is a nurturing space where learning, care, and compassion come together. We believe that every child holds immense potential, waiting to be discovered, nurtured, and celebrated. It was registered on RS/KAM(M)-02/263/415 of 2022-23 under Societies Registration Act.

At Paradise, we focus on ability, not limitation. Our dedicated team of educators, therapists, and caregivers work hand in hand to help each child grow at their own pace — building confidence, independence, and joy along the way.

Our Campus

The campus of Paradise is more than just a school — it’s a world of care, color, and calm. Designed to nurture learning and joy, our space includes dedicated rooms for every activity, from therapy and academics to art, music, and play. Children explore freely in a safe and welcoming environment, surrounded by a beautiful garden that adds life and freshness to every day. With open spaces to run, laugh, and grow, our campus reflects what we believe in — a place where comfort meets creativity, and every corner inspires progress.
